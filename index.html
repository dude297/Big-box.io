Our Products

Two systems. One mission.

Whether you need a home-based dispenser or a portable solution for on-the-go patients, we have you covered.

Home System

MedTrack Home

A countertop smart dispenser with built-in computer vision that confirms medication was actually taken — not just dispensed.

  • Machine vision confirms dose taken
  • Rotating disk dispenses up to 10 medication types
  • Time-locked compartments prevent accidental double-dosing
  • Stores CV data locally for caregiver review
  • AI-summarized intake reports for doctors
  • Life-threatening escalation to emergency services
Portable System

MedTrack Go

A smart pill bottle cap with sensors and LED alerts — the same safety net, anywhere your patient goes.

  • Lid-open sensor confirms access
  • LED turns red after 10–20 min if dose not confirmed
  • Push app notifications to patient & caregiver
  • Escalation to emergency services when critical
  • Password-protected to prevent tampering
  • Scales linearly for multi-patient care programs
Safety Escalation

Tiered response when it matters

MedTrack doesn't just remind — it responds. When a critical dose is missed, our system escalates intelligently through a layered response chain.

01

App Notification

Immediate push alert to the patient and any registered caregiver or guardian when a dose window is missed.

02

Caregiver & Doctor Alert

If unresolved, the patient's care team is notified with a behavioral summary generated by our AI intake analysis engine.

03

Emergency Services

For life-critical medications, MedTrack can automatically contact emergency services and the prescribing physician.
